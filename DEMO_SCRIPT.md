# Golf Majors Pool — Demo Script
**Target length:** 90 seconds – 2 minutes  
**Film in:** Portrait mode on iPhone

---

## Scene 1 — First Launch & Install Prompt (0:00 – 0:14)

**What's on screen:** Sign-in screen with the install prompt overlay visible.

> "This is Golf Majors Pool — a season-long fantasy pool built around the four major championships: the Masters, the US Open, the PGA Championship, and The Open Championship."

> "The first time you open it in your mobile browser, it walks you right through adding it to your Home Screen — so it runs like a native app, no App Store required."

*(Dismiss the install prompt or show the steps briefly, then land back on the sign-in screen.)*

---

## Scene 2 — Sign In (0:14 – 0:22)

**What's on screen:** Sign-in / Create Account screen.

> "Getting in takes about ten seconds — just a name, email, and password."

*(Tap Sign In with a pre-built account. Land on the standings screen.)*

---

## Scene 3 — Standings Overview (0:22 – 0:32)

**What's on screen:** Live leaderboard / standings.

> "Once you're in, you land on the live standings for the current major. Every entry in the pool is ranked here in real time as the tournament plays out."

*(Scroll the standings briefly so the viewer sees multiple entries.)*

---

## Scene 4 — Building Your Roster (0:32 – 1:12)

**What's on screen:** Pick sheet / roster builder.

> "Before each major locks, every member builds their roster right here in the app. You get the full field to choose from."

> "But before you just guess — you can actually do your homework on any player before you pick them."

*(Tap the **"i" icon** next to a player.)*

> "Tap the info icon next to any name and you get a complete player profile."

**— Season tab —**

> "The first tab shows their FedEx Cup ranking and their full results for the year — every tournament they've played, how they finished, and their earnings. Any majors they've already competed in this season are highlighted, so you can see at a glance how they've held up on the biggest stages."

*(Scroll through the season results slowly so the viewer can take it in. Point out a highlighted major result.)*

**— Career tab —**

> "Flip to the second tab and you get their career history specifically at this major — every time they've teed it up here, what they shot, and where they finished."

> "So instead of just going off gut feeling, you can actually see whether a guy has a track record at this tournament or if he's never really contended here."

*(Tap the career tab, scroll briefly to show historical finishes.)*

*(Close the player profile and return to the pick sheet.)*

> "Once you've done your research, you add them to your roster, set your tiebreaker score, and lock it in before the tournament starts."

*(Select a couple of players to show the roster filling in, then tap confirm.)*

---

## Scene 5 — Live Entry Breakdown (1:12 – 1:38)

**What's on screen:** Standings, then entry breakdown popup.

> "Once the tournament is live, every entry updates in real time. Tap anyone on the leaderboard to pull up a full breakdown of their roster."

*(Tap an entry.)*

> "This shows every golfer they picked, their current score, and exactly how many points each one is contributing to their total."

*(Tap a golfer inside the breakdown.)*

> "Tap a golfer and you can drill all the way down into their individual scoring breakdown — and even pull up a full hole-by-hole scorecard."

*(Show the scorecard briefly.)*

---

## Scene 6 — Close (1:38 – 1:50)

**What's on screen:** Back on the standings.

> "Scores update automatically throughout every round, so the leaderboard is always live. Points stack up across all four majors the whole season — so it stays competitive from Augusta all the way through The Open."

*(Hold on the standings for a beat, then end.)*

## 30-Second Version

**What's on screen:** Move quickly — sign-in → standings → pick sheet → player "i" icon → standings breakdown.

> "Golf Majors Pool is a season-long fantasy pool for the four majors. Before each tournament you build your roster right here — tap the info icon on any player to see their FedEx Cup ranking, their year-to-date results with majors highlighted, and their full career history at that specific major. Once the tournament is live, every entry updates in real time on the leaderboard — tap any team to see exactly how each golfer is scoring. Points stack across all four majors all season long."

*(Total: ~28 seconds at a natural pace.)*

---

## Recording Tips

- **Prep a half-built roster** before filming Scene 4 so you're not picking from scratch on camera — just add the last 2–3 players during the take
- **Pick a player with a strong career history** at the current major for the "i" icon section — the career tab is more visually compelling with real data in it
- **Linger on the season tab** when a major result is highlighted — that's one of the strongest features visually
- **The header color matches the tournament** (red for US Open, green for Masters, etc.) — it looks sharp on camera, let the viewer see it
- **Don't rush Scene 4** — the player profile is the most unique feature and worth giving 30–35 seconds on its own
